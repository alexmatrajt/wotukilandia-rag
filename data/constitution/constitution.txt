TITLE: Constitution of Wotukilandia
VERSION: 1.0
ENACTED: Cycle 3021
AUTHORITY: Founding Council of Wotukilandia

---

SECTION 1: FUNDAMENTAL PRINCIPLES

Article 1.1: Sovereignty
Wotukilandia is a sovereign interstellar nation governed by the rule of law.

Article 1.2: Supremacy of Constitution
This Constitution is the highest legal authority. All laws and regulations must comply with it.

Article 1.3: Rule of Law
All entities, including individuals, organizations, and governing bodies, are subject to the law.

---

SECTION 2: RIGHTS AND LIBERTIES

Article 2.1: Right to Existence
All sentient beings within Wotukilandia have the right to exist without unlawful termination or harm.

Article 2.2: Cognitive and Telepathic Privacy
No entity may access, alter, or transmit another being’s thoughts without explicit consent or lawful authorization.

Article 2.3: Equality Before Law
All beings are equal before the law regardless of species, origin planet, or biological composition.

Article 2.4: Freedom of Movement
All lawful residents may travel freely within Wotukilandian-controlled space.

Article 2.5: Right to Due Process
No entity shall be deprived of rights without fair legal procedures.

---

SECTION 3: GOVERNMENT STRUCTURE

Article 3.1: Branches of Government
The government consists of three branches: Legislative, Executive, and Judicial.

Article 3.2: Separation of Powers
Each branch operates independently and may not exercise the core functions of another branch.

---

SECTION 4: LEGISLATIVE BRANCH

Article 4.1: Assembly of Wotukilandia
Legislative power is vested in the Assembly of Wotukilandia.

Article 4.2: Lawmaking Authority
The Assembly enacts statutes, approves amendments, and oversees regulatory bodies.

Article 4.3: Passage of Laws
A law requires majority approval and formal ratification.

---

SECTION 5: EXECUTIVE BRANCH

Article 5.1: High Executor
The Executive authority is led by the High Executor.

Article 5.2: Enforcement of Laws
The Executive ensures laws and regulations are implemented and enforced.

Article 5.3: Regulatory Oversight
The Executive supervises ministries and agencies.

---

SECTION 6: JUDICIAL BRANCH

Article 6.1: Supreme Court of Wotukilandia
Judicial authority is vested in the Supreme Court and subordinate courts.

Article 6.2: Judicial Review
Courts may invalidate laws or actions that conflict with this Constitution.

Article 6.3: Interpretation of Law
The Judiciary has final authority in interpreting laws.

---

SECTION 7: INTERSTELLAR JURISDICTION

Article 7.1: Territorial Scope
Wotukilandia’s laws apply within its planetary systems and designated interstellar zones.

Article 7.2: Non-Native Entities
Non-native species are subject to Wotukilandian law while within its jurisdiction.

---

SECTION 8: AMENDMENTS

Article 8.1: Amendment Process
This Constitution may be amended by a two-thirds majority of the Assembly.

Article 8.2: Limitations
No amendment may remove fundamental rights under Section 2.

---

SECTION 9: EMERGENCY POWERS

Article 9.1: Declaration of Emergency
The Executive may declare a state of emergency under extreme circumstances.

Article 9.2: Temporary Measures
Emergency powers must be limited in scope and duration.

Article 9.3: Judicial Oversight
All emergency actions are subject to judicial review.

---

SECTION 10: DEFINITIONS

Article 10.1: Sentient Being
An entity capable of independent thought, reasoning, and decision-making.

Article 10.2: Telepathic Access
The act of reading, influencing, or transmitting cognitive processes.

Article 10.3: Lawful Authorization
Permission granted under statutes or judicial order.

---
